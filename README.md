<h1>JS binary clock made by Kent Pirma.</h1>
<p>First row: hours.</p>
<p>Second row: minutes.</p>
<p>Third row: seconds.</p>
<p>Sliders will adjust the size, position and color of the clock.</p>
<p>Click on the red play button to play music.</p>
